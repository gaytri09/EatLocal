Bajra Roti

Recipe

Bajra Roti is a nutritious flatbread made with millet flour, traditionally enjoyed with ghee and jaggery. Here’s how to make it:

  1. Take 1 cup of bajra (millet) flour in a bowl.
  2. Add a pinch of salt, and mix in water gradually to form a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each one into a flat circle using your palms or a rolling pin.
  4. Heat a griddle and cook the roti on medium heat. Flip and cook both sides until golden, adding ghee as desired.
  5. Serve hot with ghee and jaggery on the side for a traditional experience.

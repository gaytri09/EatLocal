Khichdi

Recipe

Khichdi is a comforting and nutritious one-pot meal made with rice and lentils, seasoned with spices. Here's how to prepare this wholesome dish:

  1. Rinse 1 cup of rice and 1/2 cup of moong dal (yellow lentils) thoroughly, then soak for 15 minutes.
  2. In a pressure cooker, heat 1 tablespoon of ghee, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add a pinch of asafoetida, 1 finely chopped onion, 1 chopped tomato, and sauté until softened.
  4. Add 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder, the soaked rice and lentils, and salt to taste.
  5. Pour in 4 cups of water, stir, and pressure cook for 2-3 whistles or until soft.
  6. Serve hot with a dollop of ghee and pickle on the side.

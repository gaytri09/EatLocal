Gajar ka Halwa

Recipe

Gajar ka Halwa, also known as carrot halwa, is a popular Indian dessert made from grated carrots, milk, and sugar. Here’s how to prepare it:

  1. Grate 1 kg of fresh carrots and set them aside.
  2. In a pan, add the grated carrots and cook on low heat until they soften.
  3. Pour in 1 liter of milk, stir well, and cook until the milk reduces and thickens.
  4. Add 1 cup of sugar and continue cooking until the mixture thickens further.
  5. Finish with a generous amount of ghee, cardamom powder, and chopped nuts for garnishing.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy the delicious Gajar ka Halwa!

Dal Makhani

Recipe

Dal Makhani is a creamy and buttery dish made with black lentils, popular in North Indian cuisine. Here’s how to prepare it:

  1. Soak 1 cup of black lentils and 1/4 cup of red kidney beans overnight.
  2. Boil the lentils and kidney beans until soft, then set aside.
  3. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger in a pan with butter, then add tomato purée and spices.
  4. Mix in the boiled lentils, add cream, and simmer for a rich, creamy texture.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with more cream and cilantro, along with naan or rice.
